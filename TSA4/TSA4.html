Dragonite

Type:

Dragon

Flying

Dragonite (Japanese: カイリュー Kairyu) is a dual-type Dragon/Flying Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves from Dragonair . It is the final form of Dratini. It can Mega Evolve into Mega Dragonite using the Dragoniteite.

Gyarados

Type:

Water

Dark

Gyarados (Japanese: ギャラドス Gyarados) is a dual-type Water/Flying Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves from Magikarp. It can Mega Evolve into Mega Gyarados using the Gyaradosite.

Alakazam

Type:

Psychic

Alakazam (Japanese: フーディン Foodin) is a Psychic-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves from Kadabra when traded. It is the final form of Abra. It can Mega Evolve into Mega Alakazam using the Alakazite.

Pikachu

Type:

Electric

Pikachu (Japanese: ピカチュウ Pikachu) is an Electric-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves from Pichu when leveled up with high friendship and evolves into Raichu when exposed to a Thunder Stone. It is the game mascot of Pokémon Yellow.

Charizard

Type:

Fire

Flying

Charizard (Japanese: リザードン Lizardon) is a dual-type Fire/Flying Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves from Charmeleon. It is the final form of Charmander. It can Mega Evolve into two forms: Mega Charizard X using Charizardite X and Mega Charizard Y using Charizardite Y.

Gardevoir

Type

Psychic

Fairy

Gardevoir (Japanese: サーナイト Sirnight) is a dual-type Psychic/Fairy Pokémon introduced in Generation III. It evolves from Kirlia when exposed to a Dawn Stone. It is one of Ralts's final forms, the other being Gallade.
