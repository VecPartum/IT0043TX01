Big Boss

Naked Snake

Big Boss, real name John, also known as Jack, and formerly known as Naked Snake, Vic Boss, Ishmael, Saladin, or simply Snake, was a renowned special forces operative and mercenary commander.

The Boss

The Joy

The Boss, also known as The Joy, The Mother of Special Forces, Mercury Lady and Voyevoda (Russian: Воевода, "Warlord"), was a renowned American soldier, founder and leader of the Cobra Unit, the biological mother of Ocelot, and mentor and mother figure to Naked Snake.

Venom Snake

Big Boss

Venom Snake was the code name for the medic who was the leader of the Diamond Dogs and Outer Heaven as a whole, after Big Boss went into a coma. He took on Big Boss's identity and appearance after he was awoken by Ocelot. Venom Snake was also the real Big Boss's best friend and most trusted lieutenant, as well as his most loyal follower. He was also the biological father of Solid Snake and Liquid Snake.

Solid Snake

Snake

Solid Snake, real name David, also known as Old Snake, and formerly known as Iroquois Pliskin, or simply Snake, was a former spy, special operations soldier, and mercenary. Possessing an IQ of 180 and fluent in six languages, the fame he achieved from his military exploits earned him such monikers as "Legendary Soldier," "Legendary Hero," and "the Man Who Makes the Impossible Possible." He became a living legend among the military black ops because of his deeds.

Quiet

Tixij

Quiet, known to the Soviet forces as Tixij (Russian: Тихий, "Quiet").was a female assassin during the 1980s. Originally an assassin for XOF, Quiet later defected to Diamond Dogs after being defeated by Venom Snake. Quiet wore a minimal amount of clothing at all times because she could only drink or breathe through her skin following parasite-treatment due to the serious injuries she had sustained while trying to kill Big Boss during the hospital raid; wearing too much clothing would lead to suffocation.
